Fair questions

Is it really free?

Yes. During the beta there’s nothing to pay and no card on file — your feedback is what we’re asking for. We’re building TurnDesk alongside real salons, and that’s worth more to us right now than a subscription.

How do I start, and how long does it take?

You request access with the sign-up form, we review it personally, and we send your salon’s own sign-in link. It’s not an instant self-serve start — a real person sets your salon up and helps you get going.

What happens when the beta ends?

We announce pricing before anything changes — beta salons hear it first, with advance notice. Nothing gets charged automatically because there’s no payment method on file to charge.

Do I need special hardware?

No. TurnDesk runs in the browser on what you already have — a tablet or computer at the front desk, and your technicians' own phones. There’s no proprietary hardware to buy.

What about card processing fees?

Those are between you and your card processor — TurnDesk doesn’t add a per-transaction cut of its own. During the beta, new salons start with cash/manual checkout while card-processor hookup is arranged individually.
